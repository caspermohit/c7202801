812-superfast

The Ferrari 812 Superfast (Type F152M) is a front mid-engine, rear-wheel-drive grand tourer produced by Italian sports car manufacturer Ferrari that made its debut at the 2017 Geneva Motor Show. The 812 Superfast is the successor to the Ferrari F12berlinetta.

ferrari porttofino

Ferrari Portofino. The Ferrari Portofino (Type F164) is a grand touring sports car produced by the Italian automotive manufacturer Ferrari. It is a two-door 2+2 hard top convertible. The car is named after the Italian Portofino village and also succeeds the company's previous V8 grand tourer.

GTC4Lusso T

The Ferrari GTC4Lusso T is equipped with a twin-turbo 3.9-liter V8 and a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission powering the rear wheels. Producing 602hp and 561 lb-ft it offers scintillating performance and offers seating for four and a comfortable ride in the process. Carbon-ceramic brakes, a 10.2-inch touchscreen and four-wheel steering are standard while a suspension lifter, passenger info display and sport exhaust system are notable available options. A huge range of customizations round off a supremely capable grand tourer.

488-spider
